About Us

Hello! We are Connor and Naman, the co-owners/operators of the Youtube channel Gold Brick Studios. We build Star Wars MOCS (My Own Creations) and our current MOC is a large display representing the Battle of Kashyyyk from Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith. We love creating short stopmotion projects, and hope to bring you high-quality Brickfilms in the near future. You can learn more about our upcoming Brickfilms on the Brickflms page. We intend to bring you all sorts of Lego content, so stay tuned!
